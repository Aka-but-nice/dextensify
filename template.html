Simple Chrome Extension Disabler

Instructions

  1. Open chrome://extensions
  2. Go to the settings page for this extension: {{extension_name}}
  3. Click the "create iframes" button.
  4. Back on the chrome://extensions page, click the "allow access to file URLs" switch
  5. Go back to this page and click the "create iframes" button a few times.
  6. The extension should now be disabled.

During this process, your Chromebook may freeze momentarily. This is normal, and it should resolve itself after a few seconds.
